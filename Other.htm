Hamsters

⦿ 1 - Create a suitable place for your pet so that he can rest properly, away from noise and disturbances. Also remember to put some accessories in his cage so that he can have fun .


⦿ 2 - Clean your hamster's cage so that it is clean. Remember to add food to it daily .


⦿ 3 - Ne dérangez pas votre hamster durant son sommeil, vous risqueriez de le perturber. Son sommeil lui permet d’avoir une espérance de vie plus élevée.


⦿ 4 - Faites-le sortir régulièrement de sa cage pour qu’il puisse se promener, mais veillez à ce qu’il reste loin des fils électriques, qui pourraient lui être fatal.

Rabbit

⦿ 1 - A spacious and well-appointed cage .


⦿ 2 - Games .


⦿ 3 - A bottle with clean water changed regularly A feeder .


⦿ 4 - A stick or gnawing stone that you will find in a pet store to file your teeth A harness and leash to take it out into the garden if you have one.


⦿ 5 - Une cage d’extérieure si vous avez un jardin .


⦿ 6 - Bedding costs: in shavings or mineral for the needs, and straw for the bedtime .


⦿ 7 - Veterinary expenses: if your rabbit is in good shape, he will only see the vet once a year for his vaccine and a routine check-up. The vet checks the rabbit's teeth, claws, and ears .


⦿ 8 - However, plan to put money aside to anticipate possible health costs. .
